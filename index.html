Suparosa


Jamming Since 2019
 

Suparosa was a rock band based out of Urbana, Illinois. Members included Jonathan Jacobson, Nicholas O'Brien, Ian Lopez, Daniel Holmes, Jonathan Drougas, and Marco Landaverde. Past members include Jessica and Daniel (I'm sorry I don't remember your last names!) It was active from 2019 to 2021. Check out the youtube channel HERE. Hoping to upload more photos and videos in the future! Suparosa was playing most weekends at the Ikenberry Commons.
